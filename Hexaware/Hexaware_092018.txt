1. In Selenium variables are stored in _____________
A.  storedVars
B.   storedVariables
C.    VariablesStore
D.    All of the above
Answer: storedvars
In selenium we use 2 functions for storing value into variables. store(expression, variable name) and storedvars[‘variablename’]. Therefore, as per the option only storedvars is the correct answer.
