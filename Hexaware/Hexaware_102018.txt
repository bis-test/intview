Logic: 
++a Pre Increment - increments and then uses the variable.
a++ Post Increment - uses and then increments the variable.
If you have
a = 1;
and you do
System.out.println(a++); //You will see 1

//Now a is 2

System.out.println(++a); //You will see 3
